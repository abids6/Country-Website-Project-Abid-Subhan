Welcome to Afghanistan

Afghanistan is a country in Central Asia. It has many mountains, beautiful landscapes, and different kinds of people. Some of the main ethnic groups are Pashtuns, Tajiks, Hazaras and Uzbeks. Afghanistan is also known for its amazing food such as Kabuli Pulao, Bolani, Ashak, Mantu, Kabab and a cold dessert called Sheryakh.

I am from Nangarhar, Afghanistan. When I visited in the summer of 2024 it was the best experience of my life. The people there are very humble and welcoming. They treat guests with respect and always try to make them feel at home.

What You Will Learn on This Website

History

You will learn about the long and powerful history of Afghanistan and why it is sometimes called the Graveyard of Empires. The history shows how much the people have fought to protect their land.

Population

You will read about the forty million people who live in Afghanistan and learn about the many ethnic groups, cultures and traditions that make the country unique.

Food

You will explore famous Afghan dishes and desserts. The food is an important part of Afghan culture and it brings families and friends together.

Why This Website Matters

I made this website because I want people to see the real side of Afghanistan. Many times people only hear negative things about this country. There is much more than that. There is beauty in the land, the culture and the people. I hope that when you finish reading this website you understand Afghanistan better and maybe even want to visit one day.
