Welcome to Afghanistan

The country I chose is Afghanistan, located in Central Asia. It is known for its beautiful mountains, strong culture, and diverse ethnic groups such as Pashtuns, Tajiks, Hazaras, and Uzbeks. Afghan food is also loved around the world.

I am from Nangarhar, Afghanistan. The people there are extremely humble and hospitable. When I visited in 2024, it was the best experience of my life.

What You Will Learn on This Website

History

Discover why Afghanistan is known as the “Graveyard of Empires” and learn about its powerful historical background.

Food

Explore famous dishes such as Kabuli Pulao, Bolani, Ashak, Mantu, Kabab, and Afghan ice cream (Sheryakh).

Why I Made This Website

I want visitors to understand the real beauty of Afghanistan — not what the media shows. I hope this website inspires others to appreciate Afghan culture and maybe even visit someday.
