A Long and Powerful History

Afghanistan has a rich history that stretches back thousands of years. Due to its location between Europe and Asia, many empires and invaders traveled through the region.

The Graveyard of Empires

Afghanistan earned its nickname because powerful nations failed to conquer it. The Soviet Union entered Afghanistan in 1979, but after years of fighting, they withdrew. No empire has ever fully controlled Afghanistan.

Modern Times

In recent decades, Afghanistan has experienced conflict and political changes, but the people remain strong, proud, and hopeful for peace.
