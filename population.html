Millions of People

Afghanistan has a population of about forty million people. Many people live in villages and small towns while others live in larger cities such as Kabul, Herat and Mazar i Sharif. Families are often large and many people in the country are young.

Ethnic Groups

Afghanistan has many different ethnic Groups. Some of the largest ones are:

  • Pashtuns
  • Tajiks
  • Hazaras
  • Uzbeks
  • Other Smaller Groups

These Groups can have different languages, clothes and traditions. Even though they are different they all share the same country and they are all part of the population of Afghanistan.

Religion in Afghanistan

Most people in Afghanistan are Muslim. The two main groups are Sunni Muslims and Shia Muslims. Religion is an important part of daily life. It influences holidays, family events and the way people live and treat each other.
