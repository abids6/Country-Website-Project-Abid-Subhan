Population Overview

Afghanistan has a population of about 40 million people. The population is diverse, young, and growing.

Ethnic Groups

  • Pashtuns
  • Tajiks
  • Hazaras
  • Uzbeks
  • Many smaller groups

Religion

Most people in Afghanistan are Muslim. The two main groups are Sunni and Shia. Religion is an important part of daily life and culture.
